Step-by-Step Guide to Building a Maya Temple Model (Calakmul)

\n
\n

1. Preparation

\n

Gather materials: cardboard, glue, paints, brushes, and tools.

\n
\n
\n

2. Research

\n

Study the architectural styles of Calakmul.

\n
\n
\n

3. Base Construction

\n

Construct a sturdy base using cardboard or wood.

\n
\n
\n

4. Building the Structure

\n

Use cardboard to create the temple structure, adding layers for height.

\n
\n
\n

5. Detailing

\n

Incorporate details like stairways and temple facades.

\n
\n
\n

6. Painting

\n

Paint the model with appropriate colors representing Maya aesthetics.

\n
\n
\n

7. Evaluation

\n

Review your model for accuracy and creativity.

\n
\n
\n

8. Advantages of the Model

\n

This model serves as an educational tool and a representation of historical architecture.
